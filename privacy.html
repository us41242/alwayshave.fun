Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

What Data We Collect

alwayshave.fun collects minimal data. The only personal information we store is your email address if you voluntarily subscribe to our weekly trail conditions newsletter. We do not collect names, locations, payment information, or any other personal data through the site.

Newsletter & Email

Our newsletter signup is powered by Brevo (formerly Sendinblue). When you subscribe, your email address is stored securely in Brevo's systems. We send one email per week (Friday trail condition reports). You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in every email.

We will never share, sell, or rent your email address to any third party.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics (measurement ID: G-SENVGVQJ6X) to understand how visitors use the site. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymous usage data including pages visited, time on site, and referral sources. This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify you personally.

You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Cookies

alwayshave.fun does not set any first-party cookies. The only cookies on the site come from Google Analytics, which are used solely for anonymous traffic measurement. We do not use cookies for advertising, retargeting, or tracking across other websites.

Third-Party Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to any third party. The only external services that receive data are:

  • Brevo — email addresses for newsletter delivery
  • Google Analytics — anonymous usage statistics
  • Cloudflare — site hosting and CDN (standard web server logs)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data (including newsletter unsubscription)
  • Opt out of analytics tracking

Contact

For any privacy-related questions or data requests, email us at privacy@alwayshave.fun. We will respond within 30 days.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
